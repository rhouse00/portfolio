About Me

My educational experience started with a Bachelor of Science in Business Administration Management with an emphasis in Project Management from the California State University, Chico. I was the founding member of the student organization the Project Management Group (PMG). I attended talks at the local PMI Chapter and organized numerous community outreach projects. This laid the groundwork for creating the new option, Project Management, in the business school

After college, I worked at a construction firm as the Purchasing Manager for the 9 branches in 3 states. I provided logistical support and managed vendors. I also was in charge of inventory management, shipping and receiving, project financial forecasting, and equipment and material deployment.

I decided to pursue web development as computers and technology have always been an interest of mine.

I spent 8 weeks self-learning coding on the internet. With my 5 years of experience in accounting, finance, project management, estimation, operations, shipping/receiving, and inventory management, I have more of a holistic understanding of business. This experience has provided great insight into the web development industry’s team oriented work environment which focuses on working together towards a common goal and seeing the “big picture”. , an understanding of the bigger picture will provide useful insight.

My future goals are to continue developing my understanding of the languages I have learned so far, JS / Node / React / SQL, re-certification in project management and developing the skill set to continue in this field for the long term.
